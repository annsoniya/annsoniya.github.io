I am a graduate student pursuing my PhD in systems neuroscience from the **Information Processing Laboratory** at **[IIT Kharagpur]**, under the guidance of **Dr. Sharba Bandyopadhyay**. My research asks how neural circuits encode and decode complex sound sequences, combining in vivo experiments, behaviour, and mechanistic modelling.

* **Wet-Lab Experiments:** Two-photon calcium imaging and electrophysiology in mice. Trained in stereotaxic viral injections targeting specific brain regions and vibratome-based tissue slicing for anatomical validation / downstream workflows.
*  **Behaviour (Rig + Protocol)** Built a mouse behavior training rig end-to-end and designed the behavioral training protocol for auditory experiments. Interfaced hardware using NI-DAQ, with task control and experiment logic implemented in MATLAB; conducted behaviour training experiments in mice.

* **EEG Experiments:** Created an auditory EEG task and interfaced EMOTIV Pro with a MATLAB-based task. Built an EEG data acquisition and cleaning pipeline in EEGLAB, and analysed functional connectivity and prediction-related signals.

* **Computational Modelling:** Develop recurrent neural circuit models in the Wilson–Cowan framework. Built a three-population model incorporating inhibitory interneuron dynamics to explain patterns observed in neural and behavioural data.
